The Problem

Many students struggle to find reasonably priced textbooks, calculators, flash disks, lab coats, and second-hand materials within campus. The process is scattered and time-consuming.

Our Project Vision

This project proposes an e-commerce website designed to help university students easily purchase affordable academic and lifestyle essentials. The platform aims to improve convenience, reduce costs, and create a structured digital marketplace within the university environment.
